Leap Year Checker

A year is a leap year if it is divisible by four, except that any year divisible by 100 is a leap year only if it is divisible by 400. Write a prgram that input a year such as 1996, 1800, and 2010 and display “Leap Year ” if it is a leap year otherwise displays “Not a leap year”.
